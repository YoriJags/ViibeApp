## RECORDING CHECKLIST

### Before you hit record:
- [ ] Demo Mode ON (toggle in settings — bottom of screen)
- [ ] Phone fully charged + Do Not Disturb ON
- [ ] Screen brightness 100%
- [ ] Use a clean simulator or real device (real device looks better)
- [ ] Landscape or portrait? → **Portrait** for mobile demo
- [ ] Record at 60fps if possible

### Screen recording tools:
- **iOS:** Built-in Control Center screen recorder
- **Android:** Built-in recorder or Scrcpy for PC capture
- **Simulator:** QuickTime (Mac) or OBS
- **Add voiceover:** Record narration separately, sync in CapCut / DaVinci Resolve / iMovie

### Post-production tips:
- Add the app logo in the first 3 seconds
- Use a soft dark background music (lo-fi / ambient) at ~15% volume under voiceover
- Cut hard between scenes — no fades, no transitions (urgency > polish)
- Add captions for the Oracle headline: *"Quilox will be electric by 12:30am · 87% confidence"*
- End card: static dark screen with logo + contact

### Length variants to make:
- **2:00 full demo** — for investor meetings + emails
- **60-second cut** — for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp
- **30-second teaser** — for Instagram Reels / TikTok
