Model Notes
Conservative Revenue recognition starts Month 3 after soft-launch and venue onboarding. First month free trial for all merchants — subscription billing kicks in Month 4.

Assumptions Exchange rate locked at model assumption. Brand partnerships assumed at ₦5M/quarter per brand, 1 brand from Month 9, +1 brand every 6 months. Data licensing at ₦500K/quarter from Month 9, growing 15% per quarter.

Costs Burn is pre-team expansion. Seed round (Month 10) triggers team scale-up — model shows only pre-seed phase. Infrastructure costs grow $100/month every 6 months. No founder salary before Month 6 (lean Lagos operation).

All ₦ figures converted to $ at assumption exchange rate. MRR = total of all recurring streams in a given month. Churn applied monthly to paying venue count.
