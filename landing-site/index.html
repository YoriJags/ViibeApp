The problem

Going out is a gamble.

Nigeria's scene economy is worth $5B+. Not one platform tells you where the energy is right now.

Google Maps

Historical averages. Months old.

Built for navigation, not scene energy. 'Popular Times' shows you where the crowd WAS last Tuesday — not where the energy IS tonight.

Social Media

Curated. Not live. Always late.

A highlight reel of a moment that has already passed. By the time it's on Instagram, the DJ left and the vibe shifted.

WhatsApp Groups

Fragmented. Unreliable. Too late.

One person's experience, unverified, unscaled. Your friend says 'it's lit' — but they've been drinking since 9pm. You need data, not vibes.
