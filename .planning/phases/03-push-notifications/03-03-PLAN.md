<objective>
Build the server-side notification inbox: persist every notification to MongoDB and expose a fetch-from-server API, then build the in-app notification center screen that displays them.

Purpose: On Nigerian Android devices (Transsion/Tecno/Infinix), FCM background delivery is unreliable because these OEMs aggressively kill background processes. The server-side inbox is the fallback — when a scout opens the app and checks the notification center, they see what they missed regardless of whether the push was delivered.

Output: db.notifications collection populated on every push send. GET /api/notifications API. NotificationsScreen in the app showing the last 20.
</objective>
