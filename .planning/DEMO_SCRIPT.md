# VIIBE — 2-Minute Demo Script

---

## SCENE 1 — THE PROBLEM HOOK
**[0:00 – 0:20]** *(20 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Black screen. White text fades in, one line at a time:
> "Where's the vibe tonight?"
> "Is brunch still popping at noon?"
> "Is the concert energy dead or alive?"
> "You never know. Until now."

**[VOICEOVER]**
Every day, millions of people make decisions about where to go — concerts, church programs, brunches, sporting events, clubs — and they have no real information. Just vibes. Just guesses. We built the answer.

---

## SCENE 2 — PRODUCT REVEAL
**[0:20 – 0:35]** *(15 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
VIIBE logo pulses onto screen. Cut to the app launching — the live venue map appears. Lagos. Pins glow across the city. Each pin shows a live Vibe Score: 87, 62, 41, 94.

**[VOICEOVER]**
This is VIIBE — a live social intelligence terminal for city experiences. Every venue on this map has a live Vibe Score, right now, updated in real time by people physically on the ground.

---

## SCENE 3 — LIVE VENUE DETAIL + VIBEREACTOR
**[0:35 – 0:55]** *(20 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Tap a high-scoring venue. Venue detail screen opens. The VibeReactor — a glowing collective charge ring — pulses rhythmically. Score: 91/100. Energy: HIGH. Capacity: 70%. Gate: Open. A live feed of short scout updates scrolls below.

**[VOICEOVER]**
Tap any venue and you get the full picture. The VibeReactor is the collective heartbeat — energy, crowd capacity, door status — all scored by scouts who are actually there, in real time. Not reviews. Not photos from last month. Right now.

---

## SCENE 4 — SCOUT RATING FLOW
**[0:55 – 1:15]** *(20 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Geofenced check-in triggers when scout arrives at venue. Rating interface appears: three sliders — Energy, Capacity, Gate. Scout sets them and taps "Drop the Score." Vibe Score on the map updates live.

**[VOICEOVER]**
Scouts are the sensors. Any user who shows up at a venue gets prompted to rate it — energy level, how packed it is, whether you can still get in. Every rating sharpens the score. The network gets smarter every second someone walks through a door.

---

## SCENE 5 — VIBE ORACLE (AI PREDICTION)
**[1:15 – 1:30]** *(15 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Oracle screen appears. A glowing prediction card reads: "Victoria Island peaks at 10:30 PM — Vibe Score projected to hit 88. Best window: 10:00 PM – 12:00 AM." Historical score graph below confirms the pattern.

**[VOICEOVER]**
The Vibe Oracle uses historical patterns to predict when a venue will peak — before you even leave the house. Plan smart. Show up at the right time.

---

## SCENE 6 — MERCHANT DASHBOARD
**[1:30 – 1:45]** *(15 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Cut to the merchant web dashboard. Bar charts show Vibe Score trends across the week. Footfall heatmap. A "Boost your Vibe" CTA for promotions.

**[VOICEOVER]**
And for venue owners, VIIBE is a live analytics terminal. See how your crowd energy moves hour by hour, compare against competitors, and trigger promotions exactly when momentum is building.

---

## SCENE 7 — CLOSE / INFRASTRUCTURE PITCH
**[1:45 – 2:00]** *(15 seconds)*

**[ON SCREEN]**
Return to the city map — now zoomed out wider. More cities appear: Lagos, Abuja, Accra, Nairobi. The score pins multiply across the continent. Final frame: VIIBE wordmark over a dark screen. Tagline fades in:

> "A live sensor network made of people."

**[VOICEOVER]**
We are not building an app. We are building the infrastructure layer for live human experience — starting in Africa, going global. VIIBE is the real-time nervous system that every city in the world is missing.

---
