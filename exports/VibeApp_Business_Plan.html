Executive Summary

"VibeApp is the real-time Yelp for African nightlife — with Claude AI baked in. We tell you where the energy is RIGHT NOW, and our AI tells you where it's going."

VibeApp is building the real-time intelligence layer for human social behavior in Africa. Starting with nightlife, we answer one question nobody else can: "Where is the energy RIGHT NOW?"

Unlike Yelp, Google Maps, or TripAdvisor — which show you where the energy WAS last week — VibeApp uses a network of on-the-ground scouts who report live vibe scores, crowd levels, and gate conditions from venues in real-time. On top of that live data layer, we run Claude AI to generate venue narratives, night plans, behavioral fingerprints, and merchant insights — creating an experience that doesn't exist anywhere else on the planet.

Claude AI is live and confirmed firing across 8 endpoints as of February 2026.
Vibe Brief · Roast & Toast · DNA Narrative · Merchant AI Advisor · Night Debrief · Night Planner · Oracle Premium · Feature Control Tower. All gated with graceful fallback — zero single points of failure.

Naming & Brand Strategy

"VibeApp" is already taken on Google Play Store. Publishing with this name risks listing rejection, legal trademark challenges, and weak brand identity.

NameRationaleVerdict
Gbedu ⭐ TOP PICK "Where's the gbedu?" is literally the problem we solve. Deeply Nigerian, globally memorable (like "karaoke") Highly trademarkable — nobody owns it in tech
Pulse Clean, universal, premium. Already in product language (Pulse Drops, CartelPulse) Common word — check conflicts first
Nocturn Latin root for "night" — premium, global, easy to spell Likely clear
Turnup Self-explanatory — "TurnUp: Lagos" Needs checking

Criteria for final name: Memorable (4–6 letters) · Pronounceable globally · Available on App Store / Play Store · Trademarkable · Works as a verb ("Let me check Gbedu")
