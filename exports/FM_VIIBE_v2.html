Model Notes
Billing Split iOS/Android consumer subscriptions (Vibe+) use RevenueCat + Apple IAP / Google Play Billing. Paystack handles merchant SaaS and web-based consumer subscriptions. Apple takes 15-30% platform fee on IAP — this is NOT reflected as a cost here but reduces effective consumer subscription revenue by ~17-19% for iOS users. Factor in when reporting gross margin to investors.

Infrastructure Current stack: Railway $20/mo + Vercel $20/mo + MongoDB Atlas $57/mo + Expo Push (free tier) + Claude API (Night Planner) ~$15/mo. Total ≈ $112/mo. RevenueCat is free up to $2,500 MRR, then 1% of revenue — accounted for at scale.

Cold Start Month 3 assumes geo-concentrated launch in Victoria Island / Lekki corridor with 20-30 manually seeded scouts + 5 anchor venue partnerships. Demo mode de-risks investor presentation before organic scout density. Month 3 venues = free trial; billing starts Month 4.

Detty December December 2026 = VIIBE's annual proof-of-concept event. App Store submission must be no later than October 2026 (6-week Apple review cycle). Plan accordingly.

All figures in USD at assumption exchange rate. ₦ = Nigerian Naira. MRR = total of all recurring revenue streams in a given month. Churn applied monthly to paying cohorts.
